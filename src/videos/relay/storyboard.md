# Storyboard — Relay (RelayLaunch, 1920×1080@30, 64 beats @120bpm = 960f)

Beat grid: 1 beat = 15f. Scene boundaries are HARD CUTS on downbeats.
Frame refs below are scene-local. SFX volumes relative to future music bed.

## 1 · hook — beats 0–10 (f0–150 abs)

- Intent: weary recognition. The world BEFORE — slow, grey, red.
- Terminal, tight crop, prompt `~/acme/storefront ❯`. Mid-typing at f0
  (thumbnail = half-typed command + block cursor).
- f0–38: `git push origin main` types in bursts (3–4 char bursts, 2–5f
  pauses). f38: Enter flash.
- f40–75: response lines: `Enumerating objects… done.` then CI block appears:
  `● Queued — waiting for runner   (position 4 in queue)` — red dot.
- f75–150: time-lapse: clock `00:00 → 14:32` (mono, tabular) counting with
  accelerating tick; queue position 4→3→2 only. Camera pushes in 2% on the
  red dot. Caption lower-third at f90: `every push, every day` (32px, dim).
- SFX: keycaps (soft, every burst start), terminal ding f40 (0.25),
  clock ticks f75+ (accelerating, 0.2), low drone swell f120–150.
- Copy on screen: command + CI text + caption. ≤7 words ✓.

## 2 · turn — beats 10–16 (f150–240 abs)

- Intent: dead stop. Silence before the drop.
- HARD CUT to near-black. All motion stops (rhythm signature: the stop).
- f0–10: `Your code is done.` — Space Grotesk 100px, blur-settle 8px→0.
- f45 (beat 13): `Your deploy isn't.` — same scale, the period in RED.
- f75–90: both lines hold; faint grain only. Pre-drop silence: NO SFX after
  f50. Camera drift: barely perceptible 1% push.
- Reading time: 4 words → ≥1.8s hold each ✓ (f10–45, f50–90).

## 3 · reveal — beats 16–24 (f240–360 abs)

- Intent: exhale/awe. THE zero-gap moment, on the music drop.
- f0–18: same terminal, same prompt — `git push` re-types FAST (confidence,
  8f), Enter at f18 (= beat 17.2 ≈ land Enter ON beat 18 → f30 local…
  exact: Enter keypress frame = local f30, beat 18).
- **f30: ZERO-GAP CUT** — the entire frame becomes the live site (browser
  frame, "Norra — modular shelving" storefront hero), already loaded. No
  spinner. No transition. Lime ripple expands from cursor position (signature
  2) over f30–48.
- f48–75: URL pill `storefront-7f3a2.relay.dev` pulses lime (signature 3);
  wordmark `Relay` sets in top-left of dark band, 72px.
- f75–120: hold; site breathes (1.5% drift); caption: `Push. It's already
  live.` 44px under wordmark — the logline lands here first.
- SFX: fast keys f0–18, impact (low) + whoosh-up at f30 (0.5), pulse tick
  f48/f63/f78 (0.15).

## 4 · proof-urls — beats 24–32 (f360–480 abs)

- Intent: it's not a trick — it's every commit.
- Claim (lower-third, f6): `A URL for every commit`
- Dark panel: commit list (5 rows: hash, message, author handle, age).
  Real-style messages (`fix: cart badge hydration`, `feat: quick-add to
  bag`, …).
- f12–45: lime preview pills ripple outward from newest row (stagger 4f,
  signature 2), each with `…relay.dev` and a pulse on land.
- f45–105: cursor glides to row 3 pill, hover lift, click f78 → mini browser
  flyout shows THAT commit's version (zero-gap, f78 exact).
- Camera: slow 2.5% push throughout.
- SFX: ripple ticks ×5 (0.15), click f78 (0.3).

## 5 · proof-share — beats 32–40 (f480–600 abs)

- Intent: the URL is social — review before merge.
- Claim (f6): `Share it before standup`
- Browser frame center, the Norra preview. f15: link chip flies top-right
  out of frame (sent). f24–66: two comment pins drop onto the page
  (`@mara: ship the lime one`, `@deniz: 🔥 cart feels fast`) — pins land
  with settle spring, pulse once.
- f66–120: zoom-out 6% reveals a `Approve` button sliding in; cursor clicks
  f96 → check state instantly (zero-gap).
- SFX: swoosh f15 (0.3), pin thocks f24/f48 (0.3), click f96, soft ding f100.

## 6 · proof-rollback — beats 40–48 (f600–720 abs)

- Intent: instant works backwards too. Safety = desire.
- Claim (f6): `Rollbacks in one click`
- Deploy history list (3 versions, current pulsing lime). Cursor to
  `v41 — yesterday`, hovers `Restore`, clicks **f45 (beat 43)** → ZERO-GAP:
  entire preview swaps to previous version (visibly different hero color),
  pill re-pulses, history reorders with ripple.
- f60–120: hold + push-in on the pulsing `live` dot; caption `no rebuild.
  no waiting.` (32px) f75.
- SFX: click f45 (0.35), impact-lite f45, ripple ticks f48–60.

## 7 · climax — beats 48–56 (f720–840 abs)

- Intent: scale-awe. The biggest motion of the video (the ONE hero moment).
- f0–15: preview shrinks to a lime node (continuous zoom-out, motion blur).
- f15–60: mesh assembles — nodes light OUTWARD from ours across a dark
  world-grid (signature 2 at full size); edges draw as paths; riser SFX
  underneath; counter top-right: `deploys today` ticking `0 → 14,203`
  (tabular, accelerating).
- f60–105: full mesh pulses ONCE in unison (signature 3 at full size) on
  beat 54 (f90 local) — impact SFX, music's biggest hit.
- f105–120: settle; nodes dim to 60%, ours stays lit.
- SFX: zoom whoosh f0 (0.4), node ticks (granular, 0.1), riser f15–88,
  impact f90 (0.6).

## 8 · cta — beats 56–64 (f840–960 abs)

- Intent: calm resolve. Long hold, loop-friendly.
- HARD CUT to near-black. f0–12: wordmark `Relay` 120px settles (blur 6→0).
- f18: logline under it: `Push. It's already live.` (44px dim).
- f36: `relay.dev` chip (mono, surface pill) fades up.
- f48–120: HOLD (≥2.4s). Single lime pulse traverses the wordmark at f72
  (signature 3, last heartbeat — loop seam to hook's dark terminal).
- SFX: soft resolve ding f12 (0.2). Music resolves, fade ends ON f120.
